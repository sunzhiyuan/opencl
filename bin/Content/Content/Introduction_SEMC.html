Adreno Profiler
Introduction


Welcome to Adreno Profiler!


If this is your first time using Adreno Profiler, check that both your computer and mobile device meet Adreno Profiler's requirements. You also may need to install drivers for your mobile device.


After your device is set up, you are ready to profile. Launch an embedded application, connect to it, then open a Scrubber v1.x, Scrubber v2.x, or Grapher document and begin collecting data.
